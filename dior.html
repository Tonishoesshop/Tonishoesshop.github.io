Dior x Air Jordan 1 High

If you're a fan of the Dior AJ1 High, wait until you see the Dior x Air Jordan 1 High. Undoubtedly one of the hottest sneaker collaborations of the decade, the silhouette of an Air Jordan has never exploded like this before, and it’s time for the world to take notice. Introduced at contemporary artist Daniel Arsham’s “Paris 3020” exhibition, here’s everything you need to know about Kim Jones’s latest Jordan 1 High Dior.

With a perfect color palette, the Jordan 1 High Dior is painted in a clean white and gray scheme – more specifically, “Dior Grey,” a shade exclusively used by the Parisian fashion house. The “Air Dior” branding stands out on the tongue and heel, where you’ll also find the iconic winged basketball motif embossed. Along the lateral and medial sides, an elegant lace-like Swoosh logo glides across, paying homage to the brand’s signature B-23 sneakers, which are iconic in their own right.

Classic perforations on the toe box provide excellent breathability, while underneath, the legendary Air midsole ensures unmatched comfort and cushioning. Completing the masterpiece, co-branded details are meticulously printed across the bottom of each shoe, showcased through icy blue translucent rubber outsoles, resembling a work of art in a museum.

With a limited production of only 4,700 pairs – a nod to the debut of Christian Dior’s New Look in 1947 – the Dior x Jordan 1 High is unlike anything else on the market. Whether you’re a high-level sneakerhead or a true fashion enthusiast, act fast to secure a pair before they vanish from the shelves!
